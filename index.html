Hi! I'm Hud, and I am the postdoc lead in the AI Manufacturing Center at University of Wyoming (since January 2019). Prior to that, I was a DAAD Australia-Germany Joint Research Cooperation Fellow, and defended my doctoral thesis in the School of Science at University of New South Wales earlier in 2017.

My life's motto is quoted from the Quran:

Professionally, I design and write proposals to raise money from federal agencies, develop and deploy Bayesian approaches while melding data science with materials science to accelerate materials discovery.

Concurrently, I am heavily invested in web3 - I am the blockchain backend-developer for Genius Yield, and wore many hats, including publishing whitepapers, fraud investigations to building real-time data ingestion and processing pipelines from the blockchain.
